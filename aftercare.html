After Care

After care! Your tan will begin to develop over the next 2-4 hours. I do not recommend showering for at least 6-8 hours. Do not apply makeup, deodorant or lotion until after showering. I usually recommend spray tanning in the evening then showering in the morning. If any spray tan gets on your sheets or clothes it easily comes out in the wash. Moisturize, moisturize, moisturize! Keeping your skin hydrated with a good non mineral based lotion is key to extending your tan. Note: swimming in chlorinated water or the beach will shorten the length of your tan.
